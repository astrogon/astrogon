    - Light rum

  qty:
    - 2 oz
    - 1&frasl;2 oz
    - "10"
    - 1 tbsp
    - wash

instructions:
- Gently muddle the mint and turbinado, expressing the mint oils around the glass.
- Add the rum and lime juice, dissolving most of the turbinado.
- Add ice, stir until chilled.
- Top with soda water.
